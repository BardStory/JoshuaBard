About Me

Hello! I’m Joshua Bard, a video game developer and programmer currently based on beautiful Vancouver Island, Canada. From building intricate gameplay mechanics in Unreal Engine to crafting responsive websites and browser games with JavaScript, I love bringing creative ideas to life through programming.

I’ve had the opportunity to work with a variety of modern tools and languages including Unreal Engine (C++ and Blueprints), Python, HTML, CSS, and more. While I'm currently focused on expanding my portfolio with creative projects, I’m always looking for new challenges and enjoy tackling complex problems.

Outside of tech, I have a wide range of hobbies including Dungeons & Dragons, volleyball, golf, camping, and traveling.
